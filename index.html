About Me

A designer focused on mechanics that are easy to read, satisfying to master, and memorable long after the screen fades.

I'm Ivan, a game developer and designer who enjoys turning ideas into interactive experiences. I build games in Unity, with a strong focus on clean architecture, thoughtful systems, and technical problem-solving, while also exploring level design, concept art, and 3D modeling. I enjoy working where creativity and technology intersect, bringing ideas to life through both design and development.

Beyond games, I'm passionate about storytelling through writing, worldbuilding, and music. Whether I'm sketching, playing the guitar or ukulele, or working on new stories and creative projects, I'm always exploring new ways to create meaningful experiences and grow as a multidisciplinary creator.

Character Study 2

Indianized House of the Dragon Concept Art

For this assignment, we were tasked with creating concept art for two characters from a game or television series of our choice. The process began with selecting a game or series, followed by choosing two characters. We then conducted extensive reference collection for both characters, created black-and-white renders exploring a variety of poses, and experimented with different colour schemes and palettes. After selecting the strongest combination of pose and colour scheme, we produced a final rendered illustration with an optional background.

For our project, we chose House of the Dragon as the source material, with Daemon Targaryen and Caraxes as our selected characters. We reimagined them within the Gupta period of Indian history, adapting their designs to reflect the aesthetics, attire, and cultural influences of that era before following the complete concept art pipeline.

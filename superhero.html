Character Study 1

Superhero

For the superhero assignment, we were tasked with creating 10 superhero masks and the lore behind each mask. This included establishing why the superhero was needed, providing a compelling reason for them to wear a mask beyond simply concealing their identity, and defining the goals they sought to accomplish as masked superheroes.

We also had the option of creating supervillains for the assignment. As a group, we decided to build a shared universe where all 10 superheroes and supervillains could coexist. To give the characters a sense of cohesion, we developed interconnected backstories and a common world that tied each of them together as part of the same universe.

My contribution to this project was the creation of three of the ten masks, including their concepts, backstories, and roles within the shared universe.

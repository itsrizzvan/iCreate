Environment Study 01

Dystopian India in the Age after Kalyug

For this assignment, we were given the creative freedom to design any environment of our choice, with the requirement that it be rooted in Indian culture. We chose to imagine India 6,700 years in the future, at the predicted end of Kalyug, and built a dystopian world around this concept.

As part of the project, I contributed by gathering visual references, exploring and curating mood boards, and helping refine the finer details of the city in the final render to enhance its depth and atmosphere.
